Vitamin Ventures



ABOUT CHANNELS & PRODUCTS USA Analysis INTERNATIONAL Analysis

Vitamin Ventures was founded to make lives better with their vitamins.
The company, with a global presence, manufactures and sells vitamins directly to consumers online, through select retailers and to third party wholesalers.
They are currently in their 5th year of business, and they know they are growing astronomically, but they want to position themselves to be a lasting brand.


Project Overview


Our team has been given 2 years of data to study Vitamin Ventures’ current trends. Our team will break down the analysis as follows:

  • Channels & Products – The company sells products through 4 primary distribution channels: e-commerce (Amazon and Etsy), retailers (Walmart and Target), Vitamin Ventures’ proprietary website,
    and miscellaneous wholesalers. Overall and by channel, our data analysis includes unit and
    dollar sales by month and actual product trends.

  • USA Analysis – The data provided also has location breakouts. Our analysis also gives an overview of sales and product trends for the United States which makes up 96% of Vitamin Ventures’ total company sales. Vitamin Ventures sells in all 50 states and approximately 10,000 cities.

  • International Analysis – Vitamin Ventures has a global presence selling in 81 countries worldwide. Sales and product trends are also provided here.

Vitamin Ventures Sales % Growth - YTD 2021

Vitamin Ventures Total Company Sales - 2020 through 2021
